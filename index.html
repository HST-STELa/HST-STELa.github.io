The Survey of Transiting Exoplanets in Lyman-alpha

The Survey of Transiting Exoplanets in Lyman-alpha (STELa) is a Multi-Cycle Treasury program on the Hubble Space Telescope. We aim to conduct a population-level survey of atmospheric escape in the Lyman-alpha line with 625 orbits over 3 years.
